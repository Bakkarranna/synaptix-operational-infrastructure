# Synaptix Studio · VSL Script
**Target length:** 8 minutes
**Goal:** Break the prospect out of the "bloated agency" trap and sell the "agentic speed + ownership" model.

---

## Beat 1 · Hook (0:00 to 0:30)
**Spoken**:
> "Close the endless agency meetings. Open the terminal. If you paid a traditional agency a massive retainer this quarter and your project is still stuck in staging, this is for you. Watch the next few minutes. I will show you the exit."

**On camera:** Your face, half-lit. Direct eye contact. No music. Static frame.
**Filming tip:** Sit close to a window. Light from one side. Deliver it cold.

---

## Beat 2 · Loop (0:30 to 2:00)
**Spoken**:
> "I watched founders live this for years. You pay a hundred thousand dollars for a custom build. Six months later, you have a bloated project manager, a staging site that barely loads, and a platform that is just a glorified Webflow template. You do not own the code. If they raise prices, you pay. You burn runway waiting for standard development cycles because someone built an agency model designed to bill you for hours instead of outcomes."

**On camera:** Mix of your face and B-roll (screen recordings of endless Slack threads, generic Webflow templates, or bloated Jira boards).
**Filming tip:** Capture real screen recordings of the chaos you rescue clients from. Editor cuts back and forth.

---

## Beat 3 · Pivot (2:00 to 3:30)
**Spoken**:
> "Here is what stops it. We bypass the legacy agency model entirely. I run Synaptix Studio. A lean, elite team directing AI agents to build bespoke, production-ready code. Next.js, Vite, Convex. Deployed to your own infrastructure. Portable line by line. The bloat stopped because we engineer digital infrastructure at agentic speed."

**On camera:** You at your actual desk. Terminal open.
**Filming tip:** Show a real codebase or a terminal running `npm run build`. The realness is the proof.

---

## Beat 4 · Reveal (3:30 to 5:30)
**Spoken**:
> "Here is what you get. Premium digital infrastructure that hits different. Custom cinematic websites. Full-stack web apps. AI Agent systems that automate your operations. Built and pushed directly to your own GitHub repository. You own every line of code. We do not just write code; we build operational dashboards and autonomous systems that close your highest-ticket prospects."

**On camera:** Screen recordings of your actual Synaptix Studio designs. Show the dark mode, the glowing waveforms, the high-end NVRMND aesthetic.

---

## Beat 5 · Offer (5:30 to 7:30)
**Spoken**:
> "Here is the commitment. A fixed price. No surprises. Zero hourly rates. Within seventy-two hours of kickoff, you get a complete architectural roadmap for your build. Then we execute. We cap our intake at exactly ten projects per month to maintain our speed and absolute quality. If we miss our timeline, we keep working for free. Ten slots. Premium execution."

**On camera:** Mostly face. Occasional cut to your website's 10-slot urgency bar filling up.
**Filming tip:** Longest unbroken delivery. Memorize this beat. Look straight into the lens. Conviction sells the high-ticket price.

---

## Beat 6 · Close (7:30 to 8:00)
**Spoken**:
> "Stop the bleeding. Hand us the keys. Claim your project below before this month's ten slots are taken. We engineer digital that hits different."

**On camera:** Your face, last shot. Hold the final frame for a full second after the last word.
**Filming tip:** One take. Total silence at the end.
